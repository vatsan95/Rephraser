On-device AI

Runs via Apple MLX directly on your Apple Silicon chip. No servers, no cloud, no API keys. Your text never leaves your machine.

Works in every app

System-wide via clipboard capture. Slack, Gmail, Notion, VS Code, Chrome, Discord, Pages, Notes — any app where you can select text.

Context-aware modes

Automatically picks the right tone based on your app. Slack gets casual, Gmail gets professional, VS Code gets grammar-only.

Real-time streaming

Results appear token-by-token as they generate. First token in under 2 seconds on M1, faster on newer chips.

Before / after diff view

Toggle diff view to see your original alongside the rephrased version with color-coded highlighting. Know exactly what changed.

9 built-in modes + custom

Professional, Casual, Concise, Elaborate, Fix Grammar, Confident, Empathetic, Summarize, Key Points. Create your own with custom system prompts.

Custom keyboard shortcut

Record any key combination in Settings — Cmd, Option, Shift, Ctrl + any key. Reset to default with one click.

Choose your AI model

Download and switch between Gemma 4, Gemma 3, Qwen 3, Phi-4, and Llama 3.2. All managed inside the app — no terminal needed.

Full clipboard preservation

Saves and restores your entire clipboard — images, files, rich text — through the whole rephrase flow. Nothing gets lost.

Works completely offline

Once the model downloads, zero internet needed. Airplanes, coffee shops, VPN tunnels — works anywhere your Mac does.

30-second timeout safety

If the model hangs, inference is automatically cancelled with a clear error and retry option. No frozen UI.

Launch at login + sound feedback

Starts automatically and lives in your menu bar. Optional sound confirms when text is pasted back.
