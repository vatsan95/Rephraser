Frequently asked questions

Is Rephraser free?

Yes. Rephraser is completely free — no subscriptions, no API keys, no hidden costs. The AI model runs locally on your Mac.

Does Rephraser send my text to the cloud?

Never. Rephraser runs 100% on your device using Apple MLX. Your text never leaves your Mac — not even for analytics or telemetry.

How is this different from Apple Intelligence?

Apple's Writing Tools offer 3 fixed tones, fail in many apps like WhatsApp, route complex requests to the cloud, and are blocked in some countries. Rephraser offers 9 modes plus custom ones, works in every app, stays fully on-device, includes a diff view, and is available worldwide.

Does it work offline?

Yes. After the initial model download (~0.8 GB), Rephraser works with zero internet. Airplanes, coffee shops, VPN tunnels — anywhere your Mac works.

What Mac do I need?

macOS 14 (Sonoma) or later with Apple Silicon (M1, M2, M3, M4 or later). About 0.8 GB of free disk space for the default AI model.

Can I use my own writing style or custom prompts?

Yes. Beyond the 9 built-in modes, you can create unlimited custom modes with your own system prompts to match any writing style or use case.
