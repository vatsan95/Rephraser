How it works
Three steps. Two seconds.
01

Select text

Highlight any text in any application on your Mac. Email, chat, docs, code — anything with a text cursor.

02

Press your shortcut

Hit ⌥⇧R or whatever shortcut you've configured. A floating panel appears with your rephrased text streaming in real time.

03

Accept or switch

Press Enter to replace your text, Escape to cancel. Switch modes or toggle the diff view to see exactly what changed before deciding.

Features
Everything you need. Nothing you don't.
🔒

100% On-Device AI

Runs via Apple MLX on your Apple Silicon chip. No servers, no cloud, no API keys. Your text never leaves your Mac.

🌐

Works in Every App

System-wide via clipboard. Slack, Gmail, Notion, VS Code, Chrome, Discord, Pages — any app where you can select text.

🧠

Context-Aware Modes

Auto-detects your app and picks the right tone. Slack gets casual, Gmail gets professional, VS Code gets grammar-only. Override anytime.

⚡

Real-Time Streaming

See results appear token-by-token as they generate. First token in under 2 seconds on M1, even faster on M3/M4.

🔀

Before/After Diff View

Toggle the diff view to see original vs rephrased side-by-side with color-coded highlighting. Know exactly what changed.

⌨️

Custom Keyboard Shortcut

Change the shortcut to anything you want in Settings. Record any key combination — Cmd, Opt, Shift, Ctrl + any key.

🎛️

9 Built-in Modes

Professional, Casual, Concise, Elaborate, Fix Grammar, Confident, Empathetic, Summarize, Key Points. Plus unlimited custom modes.

🤖

Choose Your AI Model

Download and switch between Gemma 4, Gemma 3, Qwen 3, Llama 3.2, and more — all managed inside the app. No terminal needed.

📋

Clipboard Preservation

Full clipboard snapshot and restore. Images, files, rich text — everything on your clipboard is preserved through the entire flow.

✈️

Works Offline

Once the model is downloaded, everything runs locally. No internet needed. Works on airplanes, in coffee shops, anywhere.

🔔

Sound Feedback

Optional sound plays when text is pasted back, confirming the action completed. Toggle on/off in Settings.

🚀

Launch at Login

Set it once, forget it. Rephraser starts automatically when you log in and lives quietly in your menu bar until you need it.

The comparison
Why not just use Apple Intelligence?

Rephraser gives you 9 modes, custom prompts, model choice, context-aware suggestions, diff view, and works in literally every app — all 100% on your device.
